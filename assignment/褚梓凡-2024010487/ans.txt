第一问：消去上下文无关文法的二义性
1.%%与* /同优先级的上下文无关文法：

<expr> ::= <expr> "+" <term> | <expr> "-" <term> | <term>
<term> ::= <term> "*" <factor> | <term> "/" <factor> | <term> "%%" <factor> | <factor>
<factor> ::= "(" <expr> ")" | number

2.%%具有最高优先级：

<expr> ::= <expr> "+" <term> | <expr> "-" <term> | <term>
<term> ::= <term> "*" <factor> | <term> "/" <factor> | <factor>
<factor> ::= <factor> "%%" <cnt> | <cnt>
<cnt> ::= "(" <expr> ")" | number
